How to Pay Maintenance of LL

That's very simple. First, go to your wallet and check if payment is available. Then follow the steps below:

Method 1:

  • Step 1: Click the "Pay Now" button on the home page.
  • Step 2: After clicking the pay button, the amount will be shown. Click the pay button again to proceed.
  • Step 3: After payment, check your transaction history to verify the status.

You can check the transaction date, status, and amount. You can also search for past transactions for reference.
